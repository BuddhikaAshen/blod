Donate Blood, Save Lives

Your blood donation can save up to three lives. Schedule your donation appointment today and become a hero in someone's story.

❤️ Eligibility Requirements

  • ✔ Be at least 17 years old
  • ✔ Weigh at least 110 pounds
  • ✔ Be in good general health
  • ✔ Have not donated blood in the last 56 days
  • ✔ Have a valid ID

What to Expect

The donation process is safe, simple, and takes about an hour. Our trained staff will guide you through a mini-physical and the donation process. Remember to eat well and stay hydrated before your donation!
